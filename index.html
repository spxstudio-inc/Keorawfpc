CIFE

CIFE focuses on advanced education, research, and training in fisheries science, including areas like aquaculture, fish nutrition, fisheries resource management, and aquatic animal health. The institute offers postgraduate (M.F.Sc.) and doctoral (Ph.D.) programs to develop skilled professionals for the fisheries sector.
